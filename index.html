About Our Group

Our Mission

The Northern Arizona AI Meetup exists to foster a community of AI enthusiasts, practitioners, and learners in the Flagstaff area. We believe in making AI accessible to everyone, regardless of age or experience level.

Our focus is on practical applications, ethical considerations, and the exciting potential that AI brings to our region's unique industries including forestry, astronomy, healthcare, and education.
