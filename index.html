About Our Group

Our Mission

The Northern Arizona AI Meetup exists to foster a community of AI enthusiasts, practitioners, and learners in the Flagstaff area. We believe in making AI accessible to everyone, regardless of age or experience level.

Our focus is on practical applications, ethical considerations, and the exciting potential that AI brings to our region's unique industries including forestry, astronomy, healthcare, and education.

Who We Are

Our members range from curious beginners to seasoned professionals. We include:

  • • Technology professionals from local businesses
  • • Students and faculty from NAU and CCC
  • • Hobbyists and self-taught AI enthusiasts
  • • Researchers from local scientific institutions
  • • Curious community members and anyone with an interest in AI!

What We Do

Our regular activities include:

  • • Monthly meetings with presentations and discussions
  • • Hands-on workshops for various skill levels
  • • Project showcases by members
  • • Special events like hackathons and field trips
  • • Online forum for ongoing collaboration
